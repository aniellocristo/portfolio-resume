About Me

From finger paint to Legos to hand drawing I have always had an innate interest in design. My talent has known no boundaries. At a young age I found myself tired with conventional sized paper and went in search of a larger canvas. This new medium of choice, I would later come to find out, was called the refrigerator door. Since then, I am ever seeking to expand my horizon, to find a path that allows me to grow.

My formal educational background is Architecture, and I do hold a Bachelor's of Science from the University of Detroit Mercy in this field. As for my web develompent skills, those were all self taught using a plethora of resources.

While it's clear my interest is in all aspects of creativity. It is worth noting I take a special interest in where it intersects with technology. My current base knowledge is in web design, but I am in a constant state of learning. I am underway with learning web development, databases, and so much more. I guess you could say I'm on my way to becoming a full stack developer.
